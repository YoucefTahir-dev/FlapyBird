# Google Play Store Listing

## App Name

Flapy Quest

## Short Description

Fly, dodge pipes, unlock skins, and beat your high score.

## Long Description

Flapy Quest is a fast, polished arcade game inspired by classic tap-to-fly
gameplay.

Choose your country, wear its flag directly on your bird, and fly through a
world of moving obstacles. The farther you go, the more the difficulty rises:
speed, gravity, and pipe spacing evolve smoothly so every run feels fair but
challenging.

Features:

- simple one-tap controls
- country selection with flags from around the world
- visible flag skin applied directly to the bird
- progressive difficulty
- local high score
- day and night mode
- unlockable skins
- sound effects and background music
- smooth 2D gameplay powered by Flutter and Flame

No account is required. The game stores preferences and high scores locally on
your device.
