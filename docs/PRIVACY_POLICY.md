# Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: 2026-07-05

Flapy Quest is an offline arcade game.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect personal data.

## Data Sharing

The app does not share personal data with third parties.

## Local Storage

The app stores game preferences locally on the device, including:

- selected country
- selected bird skin
- day/night mode
- local high score

This data stays on the user's device and is not transmitted to a server.

## Network

The app does not make network requests in the current release.

## Children

The app is a simple arcade game. If you publish it for children or mixed audiences, complete the Google Play target audience and content declarations accurately.
